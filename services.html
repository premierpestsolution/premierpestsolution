Rodent control

Rats and mice can cause serious health risks and structural damage if left untreated. We provide professional and discreet rodent control for residential and commercial properties.

Our approach focuses on inspection, elimination and long-term prevention: identifying entry points, targeted baiting and trapping, and proofing measures to prevent return.

  • Inspection: entry points & activity signs
  • Elimination: targeted baiting/traps
  • Prevention: proofing & practical guidance
  • Compliance: approved methods per Cyprus regulations
